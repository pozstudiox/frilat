Privacy & Data Policy

Your Privacy, Your Rights!

Welcome to FriLat. We know: on the internet, privacy isn’t just a legal checkbox—it’s trust. That’s why at FriLat, your privacy is not for sale, not for trade, and never an afterthought.

This page explains, in simple language, what data we collect, why, and—most importantly—how we protect it. You always stay in control.

What Information Do We Collect?
  • Account Data: When you sign up, we ask for a username, email, and password. That’s it. No phone numbers, no ID scans, no “big brother”.
  • Profile Details: You can add a name, bio, or website to share with the community. Or keep it blank—your call.
  • Uploads: Files you upload are stored securely, never shared with third parties, and can be deleted by you at any time.
  • Basic Usage Data: We log some anonymous info (device/browser type, general region) to keep FriLat fast, secure, and bug-free.
  • Cookies: Only the minimum for login/session. No advertising or tracking cookies. Ever.
How Do We Use Your Data?
  • For You, Not for Sale: We use your data to deliver features you want—like uploads, albums, and personal settings. Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.
  • Security: Passwords are encrypted using state-of-the-art methods. Even we can’t see them. File downloads are protected by random links, not guessable by anyone else.
  • Improvement: Anonymous stats help us spot errors, improve speed, and deliver better features. No tracking, just tech upgrades!
Your Controls & Privacy Tools
  • Profile Privacy:
    • You can make your account private at any time in settings.
    • Private accounts hide your uploads and personal info from everyone except you.
    • When in “Private” mode, only your avatar, username, and (optionally) bio/website are public.
  • Delete Everything: Want to leave? You can request full deletion of your account and all files with a single click, no questions asked.
  • Data Export: Download your data whenever you want. We believe in data freedom.
  • Session & Devices: Review and manage active sessions/devices for extra security.
How Are My Uploads Protected?
  • Unique download links: Every file gets a random, private download link—only you can share it.
  • File deletion: Delete uploads instantly from your dashboard. We scrub them from our servers—no backup copies.
  • Short-Lived Uploads: For extra privacy, you can upload files set to auto-delete after a chosen period (5 or 10 minutes).
Third-Party Services
  • No third-party data sharing. If we ever integrate with other services (e.g., image editors, AI), we’ll always tell you first and let you opt-in.
FAQ
  • Q: Can I use FriLat without creating an account?
    A: Yes! But some features (like album management, private uploads) need you to be signed in, so we can protect your data.
  • Q: How do I know my uploads are really gone?
    A: We remove deleted files instantly from our servers. Our system is designed to never keep hidden copies. Transparency is our policy.
  • Q: Do you use ads or sell my data?
    A: No ads, no selling, no sneaky “partners”. FriLat is supported by our amazing users and Pro memberships.
  • Q: How do I report a privacy concern?
    A: Contact us any time at support@frilat.com or via support page.
Our Promise

FriLat will never compromise your trust for profit. Privacy isn’t a feature—it’s the default. We keep things open, transparent, and always put you first.
As the FriLat community grows, our commitment to privacy only gets stronger.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this page when features change or laws require. When we do, you’ll see it here, and we’ll notify you if anything major happens. No surprises!

TL;DR: Your data is safe, private, and always in your control. That’s our promise.
